Butter Chicken

Description:

Butter Chicken (also known as Murgh Makhani) is a popular and rich North Indian dish that combines tender chicken cooked in a creamy, buttery tomato-based gravy. Here's a simple and delicious recipe to make Butter Chicken at home:

Ingredients:

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 500g boneless chicken (cut into bite-sized pieces)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmerinc powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to Taste

For the Gravy:

  • 2 tablespoon butter (for cooking)
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil (optional, can be used along with butter)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoon ginger garlic paste
  • 2-3 medium tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon Garam Masala
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (to balance acidity)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup water (or chicken stock)
  • 1 tablespoon kasuri methi(for garnish)
  • Fresh Corriander

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the Chicken:
    • In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt.
    • Mix everything well so that the chicken is evenly coated.
    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes, but ideally for 2-3 hours (or overnight) for the best flavor.
  2. Cook the Chicken:
    • In a pan or grill, heat a little oil or butter and cook the marinated chicken pieces on medium-high heat until they are cooked through (about 7-10 minutes). You can also grill them if you want a smoky flavor.
    • Once done, remove the chicken and set it aside.
  3. Prepare the Butter Chicken Gravy:
    • In the same pan, add the butter (and a little oil if needed).
    • Once the butter melts, add the chopped onions and saut√© until they turn golden brown.
    • Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for another 2 minutes, until fragrant.
    • Add the pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Stir well and cook the mixture for 5-7 minutes, allowing the tomatoes to cook down and the oil to separate.
    • Add the sugar and mix. This helps to balance the acidity of the tomatoes and gives the gravy a nice richness.
  4. Bring It All Together:
    • Add the cooked chicken to the gravy, and stir gently to coat the chicken with the sauce.
    • Pour in the heavy cream and water (or chicken stock), and mix well. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.
    • Add the kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves), and stir. This ingredient adds a distinctive flavor to Butter Chicken.
    • Adjust the seasoning if needed (salt or sugar).
  5. Finish and Serve:
    • Garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
    • Serve the Butter Chicken hot with naan, roti, or steamed rice.
